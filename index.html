Besides some traditional ways in education assistance, we can find some new ideas. Most schools in remote areas do not have very high-level teachers, which means the content of class can only focus on the knowledge in textbook. However, the knowledge out of the textbook, which can really teach students useful life skills, is limited. For example, PE lessons, moral education, financial education, labor education, are all necessary and important but may be a great gap in remote area education. This condition is a serious limitation in the promotion of quality-orientated education in remote areas. According to the result of our survey, we can see the that the real condition of network facilities in remote areas is not as bad as that in people’s common perception, instead, the most students and parents are satisfied with their network condition, and it is enough to meet the need of daily usage, including using mobile phones to view online videos, to participate in online classes, etc. We believe digital resources may provide help in improving education quality in remote areas, since “digital technology features interconnectivity, high efficiency and real-time dynamic sharing. It can quickly and efficiently aggregate scattered high-quality educational resources, transcend temporal and spatial boundaries for dissemination and sharing across institutions, regions and countries” (Huai). Based on the information above, we are inspired to stablish an online educational assistance platform, which is based on network and involves lessons recorded by students in cities. The lessons should provide knowledge beyond textbooks. We hope through these lessons, we can teach something more interesting and meaningful, which can help with the quality-orientated education in remote areas in some ways. At the same time, we hope the students in large cities who involve recording such lessons can realize the meaning of educational assistance and value the rich educational resources in cites. This program may also offer a new way to realize educational assistance.
